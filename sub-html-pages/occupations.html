Their Diet

Gorillas like a green diet that can consist of Bamboo, celery, berries, nettles, thistles and succulent herbs. They will occasionally add insects, worms and snails.

Will consume large quantities since leaves / plants offer little nourishment

Tend to choose vegetation high in protein, low in fiber

Mountain gorilla's teeth adapted for shearing leaves; strong jaws for repetitive chewing. Western gorilla teeth have enlarged post-canine dentition but more like chimpanzees Fun fact they spend 14 hours eating, they also prefer eating in the morning
